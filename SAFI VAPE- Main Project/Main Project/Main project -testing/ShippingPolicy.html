Shipping Policy

At Saffi Vape, we strive to deliver your orders quickly, safely, and efficiently. This policy outlines our shipping practices, delivery timelines, and other key information.

1. Processing Time

All orders are processed within 1–2 business days. Orders placed on weekends or holidays will be processed on the next business day.

2. Shipping Methods & Timeframes

We offer the following shipping options:

  • Standard Shipping (5–7 Business Days)
  • Expedited Shipping (2–3 Business Days)
  • Overnight Shipping (1 Business Day) – available in select regions

3. Shipping Costs

Shipping charges are calculated at checkout based on your selected method and location. Free shipping is available on domestic orders over $100.

4. International Shipping

We currently offer international shipping to select countries. Delivery time and rates vary by location and may be subject to customs duties or taxes, which are the responsibility of the customer.

5. Tracking Your Order

Once your order ships, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can use this number to monitor your delivery status online.

6. Delivery Issues

If your order is delayed, lost, or arrives damaged, please contact us at info@SafiVape.com. We’ll work promptly to resolve the issue.

7. Restrictions

Due to local regulations, we may be unable to ship vaping products to certain regions. If your order cannot be fulfilled due to shipping restrictions, we will notify you and issue a full refund.

Effective Date: May 25, 2025

For further questions, reach out to our support team at info@SafiVape.com.
